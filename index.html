MY MUSIC TASTE

I didn’t get into Jazz until my teen years when the adults around me would play different tunes or point out many modern pop music influenced by Jazz, Blues, Rap, etc. From there, I began to branch out and learn how different genres evolve, how they relate, and the ways in which they are both influenced by and influencers of culture.
